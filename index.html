Welcome to HavenfortCIC

Enhancing tenancy support.

At Havenfort CIC, we offer comprehensive support throughout the journey of transitioning individuals from homelessness to a secure and empowering living environment. Our dedicated focus is on assisting the most vulnerable in the UK by providing them with supportive housing and fostering independent growth.

With a combination of safe and comfortable housing, along with necessary care services, we aim to equip homeless individuals with the emotional and practical skills required to eventually establish and maintain their own homes.

Our mission is to support those in need by helping them find suitable accommodations while collaborating to build a promising future they can take pride in. We are committed to responsibly housing and supporting each individual, significantly contributing to improving the quality of life for already disadvantaged and vulnerable people.

Support & Benefits

Supportive

Ensuring tenancy retention

We work closely with our tenants to provide the necessary support and resources, ensuring they can maintain a stable and secure housing situation over the long term.

Benefits

Managing benefits and debt

Our dedicated team assists tenants in navigating complex benefit systems and addressing debt concerns, ensuring financial stability and reducing the risk of housing insecurity.

Higher Living

Enhancing daily living skills

Through personalized training and guidance, we help tenants develop essential life skills, such as budgeting, cooking, and household management, promoting self-sufficiency and independence.

Opportunities

Providing guidance towards education, training, and employment opportunities

We actively connect tenants with educational programs, training courses, and job placement services, enabling them to pursue meaningful careers and achieve economic self-sufficiency.

Growth

Encouraging positive decision-making and choices

By fostering a supportive environment, we empower our tenants to make informed and positive choices that contribute to their personal growth and well-being.

Community

Engaging actively in their local community

We encourage and facilitate tenant involvement in community activities and events, promoting a sense of belonging and social interaction to enhance their overall quality of life.

Welcome to Havenfort CIC

We Care About Your Well-being

At Havenfort CIC, we firmly believe that providing support to our staff enables them to deliver better assistance, following an enabling, nurturing, and strength-based approach, which they, in turn, extend to the individuals they work with. Our dedicated Housing Support Officers offer personalized, person-led support, tailored to the right time and manner, ensuring individuals can maintain and sustain their tenancy successfully.

Our ultimate goal is to create a safe and happy environment where people can thrive and reach their full potential, moving beyond survival. As a specialized provider of intensive housing management to vulnerable groups, we work with individuals facing various barriers in their lives, offering them the support they need to maintain their tenancy, acquire essential life skills for independent living, and actively participate in their community.

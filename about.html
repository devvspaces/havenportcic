About Us

Welcome to Havenfort CIC

We Care About Your Well-being

At Havenfort CIC, we firmly believe that providing support to our staff enables them to deliver better assistance, following an enabling, nurturing, and strength-based approach, which they, in turn, extend to the individuals they work with. Our dedicated Housing Support Officers offer personalized, person-led support, tailored to the right time and manner, ensuring individuals can maintain and sustain their tenancy successfully.

Our ultimate goal is to create a safe and happy environment where people can thrive and reach their full potential, moving beyond survival. As a specialized provider of intensive housing management to vulnerable groups, we work with individuals facing various barriers in their lives, offering them the support they need to maintain their tenancy, acquire essential life skills for independent living, and actively participate in their community.
